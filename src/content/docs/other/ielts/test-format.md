## Listening (40 min)
- 4 sections
  - increasing in difficulty
  - 40 questions

## Reading (60 min)
- 3 sections
  - 1-2 texts
  - 40 questions
  - increasing in difficulty

## Writing (60 min)
- 2 tasks
1. Letter (20 min)
   - Formal, informal or semi-formal
   - at least 150 words
2. Essay (40 min)
   - at least 250 words
- There is no upper limit

## Speaking (11-14min)
1. Brief Introduction and short Q&A conversation
2. Individual Speaking
3. Discussion
   - based on task 2
